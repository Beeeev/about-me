About

My name is Levi Bevins and I am currently 20 years old. I'm from Defiance, Ohio born and raised. Some things I am interested in are computers, video games, software development and technology in general. My favorite game/technology right now is my Quest 2 VR, being fully immersed in video games can make the experience so much more real.

In my freetime, I like to tinker and research anything IT related. My last couple projects consisted of fixing my moms TV and modding a Xbox 360. Im also giving IT help to family, friends, and anybody who asks for it in my freetime. I spend a lot of time watching Youtube videos about technology such as Linus Tech Tips, GamersNexus and more.
